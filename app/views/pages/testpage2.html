R&D for Vocal Artists

Partner with us to promote your work. We provide an unique way to gain new listeners and interact with your top fans. We will turn your lyrics into designs that will be developed into dope tees and other products. Once you become a partner of R&D, you will be discovered by people who love music and art.


Submit To Open Experiment

You can take that step to partner with us today by adding your top lyrics to our Open Experiment. Or inform your fans to submit their favorite lyrics of yours to our experiment. Get a significant amount of likes from the community and we will partner with you to turn your lyrics into dope gear and promote your work.

Experiment Partnerships

Got a dope music project dropping soon or recently dropped? Partner with us to develop your own experiment where you can promote your project, gain new fans, and let our community create merch inspired by your work. Email us at art@rhymesanddesigns.com for consideration.

Gain New Listeners and Fans.

R&D community is all about music and lyricism. Our community will take a listen to your work and see what you got to say. Get your lyrics on the site to showcase your new music and projects.

Promote Your Work.

With your own personal page, you can add your work on the portfolio section, upload one of your videos and pics showcasing your brand.

Merch Inspired By Your Lyrics

If the community love your work and lyrics, we will turn your lyrics into merch. Each tee and product will be a walking billboard of your music. Email us about our partnerships for your own experiment to create merch for one of your specific projects or overall brand.

Press and Social Media.

We are showcasing the work with our community everyday through press work and our social media accounts. We have done videos, articles and part of radio shows and include work from individuals of the community. We do social chats like our #RnDRhymeChat where we have a listening session and discussion of a project on Twitter. We interview, photoshoots and cool intiatives to showcase artists and creatives in the community.

Be Part of A Positive Movement.

R&D mission is to inspire passion and promote artists. Once you join the community as an artists, we will help you promote your passion and work.

Get Cash.

Agreeing to partner with us on turning your lyrics into merch means cash for you. You will get a roltaty for each product sold online and through our retail outlets.

R&D for Visual Artists

Partner with us to promote your work and gain cash. By submitting your designs to one of our experiments, you have a chance to gain recognition from our community and get cool rewards. Partner with us to gain new fans and showcase your work to many art and music lovers.


Submit To An Experiment

You can take that step to partner with us today by submitting a design to one of our open experiments. Have the best design and get cool rewards and automatic fame in the community.
